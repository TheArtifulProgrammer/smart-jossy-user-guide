What is Smart Jossy?
Smart Jossy is a WhatsApp chatbot that provides academic and research support, offering personalized assistance, answering questions from documents, and enabling interactive conversations with fictional characters for an immersive learning experience.
1. Research and Chat
  • Provides answers to questions and helps with research
  • Assists with homework and assignments
  • Explains complex concepts in a clear and concise manner
  • Offers step-by-step guidance for problem-solving
  • Provides relevant resources and references
2. Chat with Documents
  • Accepts books and documents for analysis
  • Extracts information and answers from texts
  • Saves time by eliminating manual searching
  • Delivers accurate and relevant responses
3. Chat with Characters
  • Engages in conversations with historical figures, literary heroes, and fictional characters
  • Brings beloved characters to life for interactive discussions
  • Explores different perspectives and insights
  • Provides an immersive and entertaining experience
